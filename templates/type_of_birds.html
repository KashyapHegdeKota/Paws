    African Grey Parrots

    The African Grey Parrots are the most intelligent of the parrot species and are one of the most social. They are very sweet and affectionate. However, they require a lot of love and care otherwise they will be very unhappy.

    Blue and Gold Macaw

    The Blue and Gold Macaws are a majestic species of the Macaw family. They are highly intelligent and adapt to other birds very well. The blue and gold is a fantastic bird for tricks. They enjoy being near their owners and seek out attention.

    Lovebirds

    As their name suggests, Lovebirds are known for the loving and affectionate bond that they form with their owners. They are highly active and curious. Lovebirds can also be very territorial, aggressive, and jealous if not properly tamed and worked with from a young age.
